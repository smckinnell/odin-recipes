Homemade Pizza

Description

Creating a homemade pizza is a fun and rewarding experience that allows you to customize every aspect of your pie to your liking.
Whether you're making it from scratch or using store-bought dough, this recipe is simple yet versatile. It’s the perfect meal for family
gatherings, dinner parties, or a cozy night in. The key to making the perfect homemade pizza is in the fresh ingredients, the right toppings,
and the care you take in baking.

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • sugar
  • olive oil
  • warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic powder
  • Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make the dough: Mix warm water and sugar in a small bowl, then add the yeast. Let it sit for about 10 minutes until it’s bubbly.
    In a big bowl, mix the flour and salt, then add olive oil and the yeast mixture. Stir everything together, then knead the dough
    for 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth. Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1 hour.
  2. Make the sauce: While the dough rises, heat olive oil in a pan, then add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, garlic powder, and salt.
    Let it cook for about 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Assemble the pizza: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out to your preferred
    size. Place it on a baking sheet. Spread the sauce over the dough, then add the mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake the Pizza: Bake the Pizza: Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Take the pizza out of the oven, let it cool for a minute, then slice and enjoy!
