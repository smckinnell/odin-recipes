Biscuits with Sausage Gravy

Description

This hearty and comforting dish is a southern classic, perfect for a filling breakfast or brunch. The creamy sausage gravy, rich with savory flavors, pairs wonderfully with flaky, warm biscuits for a satisfying meal thatâ€™s sure to please everyone at the table. Whether you're cooking for a special occasion or just craving a delicious homemade breakfast, this recipe is simple to make and always hits the spot.The key to the perfect sausage gravy lies in the seasoning and texture. The sausage should be browned and crumbled to add flavor, and the milk creates a creamy base that coats the sausage in a smooth, velvety sauce. The biscuits, soft and buttery, soak up the gravy without losing their fluffiness. Together, this combo is pure comfort food. Serve with a side of eggs, crispy bacon, or fresh fruit for a complete meal that will leave everyone satisfied and ready to take on the day.

Ingridients

  • Pure pork sausage
  • Oven bake biscuits
  • Milk or cream
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook 1 lb of breakfast sausage in a skillet over medium heat, breaking it apart as it browns.
  2. Once browned, sprinkle 2 tbsp of flour over the sausage and stir to combine, cooking for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Slowly add 2 cups of milk, stirring constantly until the gravy thickens, about 5 minutes.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes, to taste.
  5. Serve the gravy over freshly baked biscuits.
  6. Enjoy with your favorite sides or on its own!
