Grilled Cheese

Description

To make the perfect grilled cheese, start by buttering two slices of your favorite bread on one side. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place one slice of bread, butter-side-down, onto the pan. Layer on your choice of cheese cheddar is a classic, but you can mix in Swiss, mozzarella, or any other cheese that melts well. Top with the second slice of bread, butter-side-up. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, pressing lightly with a spatula, until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is beautifully melted inside. Keep an eye on the heat to avoid burning the bread before the cheese melts completely. Once done, remove from the pan, slice it up, and enjoy the warm, cheesy goodness!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread butter across the outer layer of 2 slices of bread
  2. Thinly slice enough cheese to cover the inner layer of bread
  3. Put the other peice of buttered bread on top with butter sides facing out
  4. Preheat pan or skillet for a couple minutes then place the sandwich down
  5. Cook both sides evenly for 3~4 minutes or until perfectly golden
  6. Turn off the grill and serve with a side of delicious tomato soup
